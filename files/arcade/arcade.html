Welcome To My Frogger Game

How to play

Use the arrow keys to go up, down, left and right


Basic Rules

In this game you have a Player and Enemies (Bugs). The goal of the player is to reach the water, without colliding into any one of the enemies. The player can move left, right, up and down. The enemies move in varying speeds on the paved block portion of the scene. Once a the player collides with an enemy, the game is reset and the player moves back to the start square. Player can collect stars along the way. Once the player reaches the water the game is won.
